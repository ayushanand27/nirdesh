Securities and Exchange Board of India
Circular No. HO/47/11/11(1)2026-MRD-POD3/I/13804/2026
Date: June 15, 2026

To
All Recognised Stock Exchanges
All Asset Management Companies (AMCs) / Mutual Funds offering Exchange Traded Funds

Subject: Norms for base price and price bands for units of Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs)

1. Applicability and effective date
1.1 This circular shall come into effect from September 1, 2026, unless otherwise
    specified for a particular clause herein.

2. Base price
2.1 With effect from September 1, 2026, the base price for units of an ETF shall be
    determined on the basis of the T-1 closing price, computed as the volume weighted
    average price (VWAP) of the last 30 minutes of trading on the previous trading day.
2.2 The erstwhile practice of using the T-2 day Net Asset Value (NAV) as the base
    price shall stand discontinued from the effective date.

3. Price bands
3.1 Equity and Debt ETFs: A dynamic price band of +/- 10% shall apply. On triggering
    the band, a flexing of an additional 5% shall be permitted, subject to a maximum
    of 2 flexes, so that the band shall not exceed +/- 20%.
3.2 Overnight ETFs: A fixed price band of +/- 5% shall apply. This is unchanged from
    the existing norm.
3.3 Liquid ETFs: A fixed price band of +/- 5% shall apply, replacing the erstwhile
    flat +/- 20% band.
3.4 Gold and Silver ETFs: A dynamic price band of +/- 6% shall apply, with flexing of
    an additional 3% each time the band is triggered at 5.90%, with no upper cap on the
    number of flexes.

4. Transition
4.1 AMCs shall put in place systems to comply with the price band norms in Clause 3 by
    the effective date of September 1, 2026.
4.2 Stock exchanges shall make consequential amendments to their bye-laws.
4.3 This circular is issued in exercise of powers conferred under the SEBI Act, 1992.
4.4 As a second phase, AMCs shall migrate the base price computation to the T-1 closing
    NAV of the ETF by April 1, 2027, superseding the T-1 closing VWAP methodology
    specified in Clause 2.1 for the purpose of base price determination.
